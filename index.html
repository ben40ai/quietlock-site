Privacy at a glance

Quiet Lock is built offline-first: your prayer flow and journaling stay on your device. We don’t require an account for core use. When you use Apple’s Screen Time / Family Controls APIs, selections are handled under Apple’s privacy model—we work with the system’s opaque tokens to enforce what you choose, not to read your browsing history.
